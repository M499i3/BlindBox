## Brand & Style

The brand identity centers on the "unboxing moment"—the tension between high-end sophistication and the playful joy of a surprise. It targets Gen Z and Millennial collectors who value curation as much as the items themselves.

The design style is **Minimalist with Tactile accents**. It leverages heavy whitespace and a restricted palette to allow the colorful, high-detail collectible photography to remain the focal point. While the layout is clean and grid-locked, the use of oversized radiuses and subtle depth creates a "toy-like" physical quality that feels premium and touchable. The UI acts as a gallery frame: invisible enough to let the product shine, but distinct enough to feel like a high-end lifestyle brand.

## Colors

- **Primary Blue (#0047AB):** Used for core branding, primary actions, and "verified" status indicators.
- **Accent Orange (#FF8C00):** Reserved for "Surprise," "New," and "Rare" moments.
- **Soft Surface (#F0F5FF):** Acts as the secondary background layer.
- **Text:** Deep navy-black for primary text; muted variant for metadata.

## Typography

Use **Plus Jakarta Sans**.

- **Headlines:** 800 / 700 with tighter letter spacing.
- **Body:** 500 for readability on mobile.
- **Labels:** Uppercase used sparingly for tags.

## Layout & Spacing

- **Rhythm:** 8px base grid.
- **Margins:** 20px side margin for mobile.
- **Gutters:** 16px in 2-column grids.

## Elevation & Depth

Use **Ambient Shadows and Tonal Layering** (soft, diffused shadow + subtle inner stroke). On press, scale to ~98% and tighten shadow.

## Shapes

- **Cards:** 24px radius.
- **Buttons:** pill radius.
- **Inputs:** 16px radius.
